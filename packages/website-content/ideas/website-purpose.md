# Website Purpose (Draft)

> **Draft notes — not requirements.** Translated from a spoken brainstorming
> transcript and lightly structured.

## Context

We are founding a non-profit association (Verein) for knowledge exchange about
Software Factories and Agent-OS systems, with a focus on professional, subject-matter
exchange. For this non-profit we want to build a website.

## Purpose of the website

The website should serve the following purposes:

- **Member content:** Provide content to members in a closed members-only area.
- **Member acquisition:** Support recruiting new members, including an online
  application/join flow.
- **Online payment:** Allow membership fees to be paid online.
- **Self-presentation:** Offer a small public presentation of the association.
- **Public content area:** Communicate (less deeply than the members' content) all
  the topics and events involved, so visitors are teased into participating or
  attending events.

## Required capabilities (high level)

- Registration and a login area.
- Public content and non-public (members-only) content.
- Tracking that is well suited to online campaigns.

## Audience

We want to use our content to reach and engage new members and users.
