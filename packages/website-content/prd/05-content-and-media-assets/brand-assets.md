## Canonical source

Brand assets (logo, typography, guidelines, mood board) are owned by the
**`brand-kit`** package in `aoc-foundations`, not duplicated here:

- `aoc-foundations/packages/brand-kit/logo/`
- `aoc-foundations/packages/brand-kit/guidelines/`
- `aoc-foundations/packages/brand-kit/mood-board/`
- `aoc-foundations/packages/brand-kit/drafts/`

See [Sources & References](../sources-and-references.md) for the full registry of
canonical sources.

## Usage on the website

- The site must honor **browser theme signals** (dark/light, high contrast) — see
  [Non-Functional Requirements](../04-technical-specs-and-quality/non-functional-requirements.md).
- Member and sponsor **logos** are sourced per the
  [member & sponsor directory epic](../03-functional-requirements/epics/member-and-sponsor-directory.md)
  (APIs such as Gravatar/LinkedIn) rather than stored here.
