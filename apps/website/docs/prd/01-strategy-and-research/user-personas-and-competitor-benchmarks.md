## Audience

We want to use our content to reach and engage **new members and users** for the
association, in the domain of Software Factories and Agent-OS systems.

## Membership categories (from the statutes)

The membership form and fees differ by category. These double as the primary
personas to design for:

> Single source of truth: `aoc-governance/packages/statutes` (see
> [Sources & References](../sources-and-references.md)).

| Category | Notes |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Researcher** | Academic / research-affiliated individuals |
| **Public authority** | Public-sector organisations |
| **Company** | Differentiated by company size; fee varies by size |
| **Individual** | Private individuals |

Each category sees the **correct annual fee** and the relevant **benefits**
immediately during application.

## Provisional personas

- **Prospective member** — discovers the association (often via LinkedIn), explores
  public content and events, and applies to join.
- **Pre-known member** — already onboarded offline / invited; registers online and
  is immediately recognised as a member.
- **Active member** — logs in for member-only content and event documentation; may
  contribute talks and professional articles.
- **Sponsor** — an organisation funding event formats in exchange for visibility and
  access to member events.

## Member motivations (why join)

- Be **visible as a member** and use the membership for own marketing.
- Preferred opportunity to **contribute talks** at events as an expert.
- **Participate in the association's bodies**, including the curatorium (which owns
  the subject-matter direction and curation of events) — i.e. have a say.
  - Bodies and roles are defined in the bylaws: `aoc-governance/packages/bylaws`
    (see [Sources & References](../sources-and-references.md)).
- **Access member-only event content.**
- **Exchange with other experts** — happens off-site (e.g. WhatsApp communities or
  LinkedIn), not via an on-site forum.

## Sponsor motivations

- **Logo on the website.**
- **Named as sponsor at events.**
- Right to **attend member events** with knowledgeable people.
