# Business Case & KPIs

> 🟡 **DRAFT** — Derived from the website purpose draft. Not approved.

## Context

We are founding a non-profit association (Verein) for knowledge exchange about
**Software Factories and Agent-OS systems**, with a focus on professional,
subject-matter exchange. The website is the digital home of this association.

## Why the website exists (business case)

The website serves five core purposes:

- **Member content** — provide content to members in a closed members-only area.
- **Member acquisition** — recruit new members, including an online
  application/join flow.
- **Online payment** — allow membership fees to be paid online.
- **Self-presentation** — offer a public presentation of the association.
- **Public content area** — communicate the topics and events (less deeply than the
  members' content) so visitors are teased into participating or attending events.

## Strategic goals

- Reach and engage **new members and users** through content.
- Generate reach, likely with heavy use of **LinkedIn** as a distribution channel.
- Fund event formats and high-level business exchange via **memberships and
  sponsorships**.

## KPIs (proposed, to be refined)

| Goal | Candidate KPI |
| :---- | :---- |
| Member acquisition | Applications submitted; applications approved; activation (paid) rate |
| Reach | Website visitors; traffic by source; LinkedIn referral traffic |
| Campaign performance | Conversions per campaign; cost per acquisition (if ads run) |
| Payment | Fees paid online; time from approval to payment |
| Sponsorship | Sponsor sign-ups; sponsor-funded event budget |
| Engagement | Members-area logins; content views |
